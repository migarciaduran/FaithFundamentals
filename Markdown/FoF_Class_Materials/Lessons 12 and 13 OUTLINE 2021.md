FOF Lessons 12 & 13 – Obedience and God’s Will - OUTLINE

1.  Introduction

2.  What is Obedience?

    1.  Obedience is not just a list of dos and don’ts – Colossians
        2:20-23; Romans 14:17-18

    2.  Obedience is a heart issue – Psalm 40:8; Ephesians 6:5-6

    3.  Obedience is about denying self – Luke 9:23

    4.  Obedience is about offering ourselves to God – Romans 12:1

    5.  Obedience is about personal holiness – 1 Peter 1:16; Hebrews
        12:14

3.  Why do we need to be Obedient?

    1.  We are called to love God – Mark 12:28-31

    2.  We show our love to God by keeping His commandments ; John
        14:15, 21, 23; 2 John 6a

    3.  Scripture commands obedience of believers – Ecclesiastes 12:13

4.  How do we know what to obey? How do we know what God’s Will is?

    1.  Different Aspects of God’s Will

        1.  <u>God’s Sovereign/Decreed Will</u> – Isaiah 14:24; Acts
            2:22-23whatever God proposes will happen

        2.  <u>God’s Will of Desire</u> – 1 Timothy 2:3-4; 2 Peter 3:9;
            Matthew 23:37

        3.  <u>God’s Commanded or Moral Will</u> – Ephesians 5:17;
            Colossians 1:9

    2.  Read and Study Scripture noting its Commands – Psalm 119:105;
        Psalm 119:11; James 1:22-25

**HANDOUTS**

5.  How do we obey?

    1.  Heart Issues Pertaining to Obedience

        1.  Willingness – John 7:17

        2.  Trusting in the Lord – Proverbs 3:5-6

        3.  Praying for Wisdom – James 1:5

        4.  Seeking Godly Counsel – Proverbs 1:5

    2.  Practical Pointers to Help with our Obedience

1.  Imitate Christ – Ephesians 5:1-2

2.  Set our minds on things above – have a heavenly perspective –
    Colossians 3:1-2

3.  Fix our eyes on Jesus – Hebrews 12:1-2
