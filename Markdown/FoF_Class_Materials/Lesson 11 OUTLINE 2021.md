FOF Lesson 11 – Evangelism

1.  Introduction

    1.  The Narrow Gate – Matthew 7:13-14

    2.  Hell – Luke 16:19-31; Matthew 13:41-42; Revelation 20:10, 21:8

    3.  Exhortation/Conviction – Matthew 9:37

2.  Having a Heart for the Lost

    1.  The Lord had a heart for the lost – Luke 19:10; Luke 19:41

    2.  Paul had a heart for the lost – 2 Corinthians 12:15

    3.  The Lord desires all to be saved – 2 Peter 3:9; 1 Timothy 2:3-4

3.  The Command to Witness – Matthew 28:19-20; Acts 1:8

4.  Hindrances to Witnessing

    1.  Fear

<!-- -->

1.  Pray for Boldness – Acts 4:29

2.  Realize that the Gospel comes with power – Romans 1:16

3.  Realize that we witness in the wisdom and power of God – 1
    Corinthians 2:3-5

    1.  Lack of Training

        1.  Jesus trained His disciples to witness – Matthew 10:5

        2.  Be ready to give an account – 1 Peter 3:15

<!-- -->

5.  Content of Our Witness

    1.  Our Own Life/Testimony – Philippians 2:14-15; Matthew 5:14-16

    2.  The Message – 1 Corinthians 15:3-4; Romans 10:9-10

    3.  Points to Cover When Presenting the Gospel – Romans 3:23; Isaiah
        59:1-2; Hebrews 9:27; 2 Thessalonians 1:8-9; Romans 6:23; 1
        Peter 3:18; Romans 5:8; Ephesians 2:8-9; Romans 10:9

6.  Things to Consider When Witnessing

    1.  Know the message

    2.  Pray for salvation for others; that their hearts might be opened
        – Rom 10:1; Eph 1:18

    3.  Pray for an open door and clear speaking – Col. 4:3-4

    4.  Pray that God would give you the words – Eph 6:19

    5.  Use the Word of God – Hebrews 4:12 (sword); 2 Tim. 3:15
        (Scripture leads to salvation)

    6.  Share your testimony – very powerful; no one can argue with it –
        make sure your life matches it

    7.  Elicit a response – ask if the person is willing to turn from
        their sin and to Christ; ask if they are wiling to embrace
        Christ as Lord

    8.  Practical

        1.  Be a good listener

        2.  do not become defensive

        3.  don’t argue

        4.  don’t be pushy

        5.  do not move beyond their understanding

        6.  if you do not know something – say you don’t and find out

        7.  know your message and stay on it.

        8.  Christ is the focus.
